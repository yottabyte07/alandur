Section 01

Constituency Profile & Ground Context

A 100% urban constituency at the crossroads of old Chennai and the IT-driven new city — full of aspirations, and full of unresolved civic grief.

📂 Key Contextual Facts
  • Peripheral localities still neglected: Periyapanicheri, Paraniputhur, Kovur, Thandalam — still lack reliable piped water and UGD as of March 2026 (The Hindu, Mar 2026).
  • DVAC HQ is in Alandur — Tamil Nadu's anti-corruption agency HQ sits in the very constituency whose MLA was raided by them for disproportionate assets in 2011.
  • NTK polled ~7% in 2021 — a real, buildable voter base. Combine with disenchanted AIADMK voters (32%) for a genuine breakthrough path.
  • AMRUT 2.0 / TNCRUDP funds sanctioned for Kancheepuram area — yet peripheral Alandur pockets remain excluded. Money was there. Delivery was not.
🗺️

Areas Covered

Alandur, Nanganallur, Adambakkam, Palavanthangal, Moulivakkam, Nandambakkam, Iyyappanthangal, Manapakkam, Mugalivakkam, Cowl Bazaar, St. Thomas Mount, Gerugambakkam, Kolapakkam, Periyapanicheri, Paraniputhur, Kovur, Thandalam.

👥

Voter Demographics

~3,83,509 electors (2024). Lower-middle class families, factory/daily-wage workers, auto drivers, domestic workers + IT professionals near OMR/ECR. SC: 16.85%. 100% urban. Merged into GCC 2011/2014 — yet still fighting for basics.

📍

Strategic Location

4 km from Chennai Airport. Borders Guindy industrial hub. Sriperumbudur Lok Sabha (MP: T.R. Baalu, DMK). Proximity to IT corridors has pushed up cost of living — without improving civic services for long-time residents.

Section 05

Top 10 Open Issues — Alandur 2026

Ranked by severity, voter impact, and electoral resonance. Synthesized from Claude + ChatGPT + Grok research.

🌊

1. Chronic Monsoon Flooding

Adambakkam, Nanganallur, Palavanthangal, Meenambakkam subways flood every year. Dec 2024 rains caused sewage overflow and road flooding. Porur Lake canal widening — promised 2021 — unimplemented.

🚰

2. Drinking Water Crisis

Periyapanicheri, Paraniputhur, Kovur, Thandalam still on tankers/borewells (March 2026). Nov 2024: residents petitioned GCC for broken water pumps. AMRUT funds sanctioned but peripheral areas excluded.

⚡

3. Sewage & UGD Gaps

Open drains and sewage overflow in Iyyappanthangal-Gerugambakkam-Periyapanicheri cluster. Residents paying sewage taxes while hiring lorries. Sewage mixes into stormwater drains — disease risk every monsoon.

🏞️

4. Adambakkam Lake Destruction

80 acres → 8 acres. Natural flood buffer destroyed by 20 years of political inaction. Restoration stuck in inter-departmental funding disputes since 2006. Encroachments continue.

🚌

5. Public Transport Failure

No mini-bus/shared-auto in Nanganallur interior, Mugalivakkam-Mount Poonamallee. Last-mile Metro connectivity absent. 2 MRTS stations incomplete since 2016. Working-class commuters worst affected.

🛣️

6. Road Congestion & Potholes

Narrow interior roads, Mugalivakkam Bypass poor condition, GST Road Airport zone congestion. Rapid urbanization without matching road infra. Bengaluru-Chennai Expressway worsens local load.

🏥

7. Healthcare Overload

Population tripled in 20 years. Government hospital unchanged. Urban poor and daily-wage workers face dangerously overcrowded facilities. No new public health facility announced for constituency.

👷

8. Local Employment Gap

IT corridor surrounds Alandur but local youth don't benefit. No MSME cluster, no skilling centre. Anbarasan is now MSME Minister — zero local implementation in 4 years of power.

🗑️

9. Solid Waste & Sanitation

Irregular GCC collection, overflow bins, unsegregated waste near drains — worsening flooding and disease. Modernization promised 2021. Still waiting after GCC merger in 2011.

🎓

10. Education & NEET Stress

NEET disadvantages Tamil-medium government school students. DMK promised abolition — failed to deliver. Working-class Alandur families who invested everything face a broken system.
